<div align="justify">
  
I am a Senior Data Scientist at the [CDC](https://www.cdc.gov) focusing on developing models and tools for the detection of pathogens in wastewater. I am a former Presidential Management Fellow (PMF Class of 2023) and served as a Physical Scientist at the [EPA](https://www.epa.gov).

I hold a Ph.D. from [Vanderbilt University](https://www.vanderbilt.edu), where my research quantified the threat of sea-level rise to tidal deltas like the Ganges-Brahmaputra-Meghna through field-based methods and numerical modeling. This work culminated in the open-source software [tidal_flat](https://github.com/christasich/tidal_flat), which received 2nd place in the [2023 Syvitski Student Modeler Award](https://csdms.colorado.edu/wiki/Awards).
